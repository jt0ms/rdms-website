Our Mission & Vision

RDMS serves as a vital bridge between clinical dental medicine, academic research, and grassroots community outreach. We believe that oral health is a fundamental pillar of general well-being.

Through our integrated approach, we empower dental students and professionals to lead impactful screening programs and contribute to global dental science from a Rwandan perspective.
